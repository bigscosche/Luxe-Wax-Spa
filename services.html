Before You Book

Good to Know

Cancellation Policy

Please provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. Your time — and ours — is valued.

Prep for Your Wax

Hair should be at least ¼ inch long (about 10–14 days of growth). Exfoliate gently 24 hours before, and avoid tanning or sun exposure for 24 hours post-wax. Come hydrated!

First Time? Welcome.

First Brazilian? No worries. Cida takes extra care to explain each step, answer your questions, and keep the experience as comfortable as possible. Everyone starts somewhere.

Ready to book?

Online booking is available 24/7. No phone calls needed — just pick your service and go.
