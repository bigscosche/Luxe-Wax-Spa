First Time?

Everything you need
to know before you book.

First-time Brazilian wax clients are welcome at Luxe Wax Spa. The most common experience: clients come in nervous and leave wondering what they were worried about. Cida has guided hundreds of first-timers through the process — the environment is calm, the explanation is clear, and the technique is designed to minimize discomfort.

How long should hair be?

About a quarter inch — roughly 2–3 weeks of growth after shaving. Not too long, not too short. If you're unsure, err on the side of more growth.

Does it hurt?

There is some discomfort — but most clients are surprised by how manageable it is. The hard wax technique and heated table reduce it significantly. First visits are typically the most sensitive; repeat visits are easier.

How long does it take?

A full Brazilian typically takes 30–45 minutes. Cida does not rush. The thoroughness and the vajacial are part of every appointment.

How long do results last?

Most clients find results last 4–6 weeks. With regular waxing, hair tends to grow back finer and sparser over time — many clients notice this within a few months of consistent appointments.

Do I need to do anything to prepare?

Make sure hair is the right length, avoid retinoids and exfoliants on the area for 48 hours beforehand, and wear comfortable, loose clothing. That's it.
