Meet Cida,
Founder

Cida is a Connecticut-licensed esthetician with over 8 years of waxing expertise. What began with a passion for making clients feel genuinely cared for has grown into Luxe Wax Spa — a private, peaceful studio at 125b S Main St in Newtown.

Every treatment is done by Cida personally. One client at a time, no front desk, no waiting. Clients travel from Danbury, Bethel, Ridgefield, and Monroe for results that keep them coming back.
