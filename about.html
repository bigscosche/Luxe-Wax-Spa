Her Story

Rooted in care.
Driven by beauty.

I was born in Brazil and have called the United States home for over 26 years. Beauty has always been a passion — but it was waxing that truly captured my heart. What began as a skill became a calling, and over the past 8+ years I've dedicated myself to mastering the craft.

As a mom of three and grandma of one, I understand what it means to be busy, to want to feel your best, and to need an appointment that fits your life. That's why I built Luxe Wax Spa around comfort, efficiency, and a genuinely warm welcome — every single time.

At 49, I bring both experience and empathy to every service. Whether it's your very first Brazilian or your tenth lash lift, you'll leave feeling like the most polished version of yourself.

Your First Visit

What to expect

First time here — or first time ever? Either way, you're in good hands. Here's exactly what happens when you arrive.

01

You arrive, you relax

The suite is private — there's no waiting room, no other clients, no front desk. You'll come directly to a calm, clean space that feels more like a personal studio than a salon.

02

Cida checks in with you

Before anything begins, Cida takes a moment to understand your skin, your history with waxing, and any sensitivities. No question is off-limits. First Brazilian? She's guided hundreds of first-timers — you'll feel at ease immediately.

03

The treatment is unhurried

Your appointment is yours alone. Cida works carefully, methodically, and never rushes. The heated table, premium products, and low-temp hard wax technique are all designed to keep you comfortable throughout.

04

You leave feeling polished

Results are thorough. Aftercare instructions are shared before you go. Most clients book their next appointment before leaving — not because they have to, but because they want to.
