Her Story

Rooted in care.
Driven by beauty.

I was born in Brazil and have called the United States home for over 26 years. Beauty has always been a passion — but it was waxing that truly captured my heart. What began as a skill became a calling, and over the past 8+ years I've dedicated myself to mastering the craft.

As a mom of three and grandma of one, I understand what it means to be busy, to want to feel your best, and to need an appointment that fits your life. That's why I built Luxe Wax Spa around comfort, efficiency, and a genuinely warm welcome — every single time.

At 49, I bring both experience and empathy to every service. Whether it's your very first Brazilian or your tenth lash lift, you'll leave feeling like the most polished version of yourself.

The Luxe Difference

Why Clients
Choose Cida

Near-Painless Technique

Cida uses a refined, low-temperature hard wax method that adheres to hair — not skin. Clients who dreaded waxing elsewhere describe their first visit here as "almost painless." Sensitive skin is her specialty, not her obstacle.

Gentle & Effective
02

Heated Table & Premium Products

Your comfort is built into the suite itself. A heated waxing table keeps you relaxed from start to finish. Every service uses high-quality, skin-safe products selected to minimize irritation and maximize results — especially for sensitive skin types.

Comfort First
03

Private & Judgment-Free

One client at a time. One esthetician — always Cida. Your appointment is yours alone, in a suite designed to feel calm, clean, and completely private. No front desk, no waiting room chatter. Just a warm, welcoming space where every body is celebrated.
