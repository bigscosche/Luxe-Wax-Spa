Before You Arrive

A quick prep guide

Everything you need to know so your appointment goes smoothly from the moment you walk in.

01
Hair length

At least ¼ inch of growth — roughly 10–14 days after shaving. A little extra is always better than too short.

02
Exfoliate & hydrate

Gently exfoliate 24 hours before. Arrive with clean, dry skin. Drink water — hydrated skin responds better to wax.

03
Avoid sun & heat

Skip tanning beds, sunbathing, and hot tubs for 24 hours before and after your service. Freshly waxed skin is more sensitive.

04
Skip the gym after

Avoid intense workouts and saunas for 24 hours post-wax. Sweat and friction can irritate freshly treated skin.
