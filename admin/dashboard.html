Quick actions

Start here when you need to enter something manually. Completed appointments handle normal service revenue automatically, so manual transactions are mostly for exceptions.

Add Appointment Book a client and put them on the calendar. Add Client Create a client record before booking services. Add Inventory Item Track wax, supplies, and reorder thresholds. Log Income / Expense Record sales, supplies, and other business activity.

Today's workflow

Follow this order to keep the day moving and reduce extra admin work.

1. Book appointments Add the client if needed, pick the service, and let the default price fill in for you.
2. Complete services Mark finished appointments as completed and the system will log normal service revenue automatically.
3. Log exceptions manually Use Transactions for rent, supplies, software, refunds, product sales, or anything outside the standard appointment flow.
